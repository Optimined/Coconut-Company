Core Production Process

From shell to carbon,
a precise journey.

A mature, precise process with layered checks from raw materials to finished products — so every batch meets strict quality standards.

  1. Step 01

    Raw Material Screening

    Select high-quality coconut shells and strictly control the source and quality of raw materials.

  2. Step 02

    Carbonization

    High-temperature drying removes volatile components to form dense carbonized material.

  3. Step 03

    Post-treatment

    Water cooling and fine screening to ensure consistently high purity.

  4. Step 04

    Packaging

    Industrial-grade, moisture-proof, fixed packaging to ensure safe transport and storage.
